About

Welcome to my archive of music ventures thus far.

Bands

LOWER LOVEDAY

As the bassist, I took part in some of the biggest gigs of my music career. I was not involved in the production of the music, but I was involved in some of the development of ideas in the live performances of later singles. The band was founded by the drummer Tom Garbett and Mark Washington. Chris Francis, the guitarist, joined at the same time as me, but knew the other members previously. The band is based in Evesham, so we endeavoured to play as many gigs as we could in the surrounding area. My last gig with the band was with on the 10th September 2018, supporting Only The Poets at the Louisiana.



SUBTLE CULPRITS

Being the bassist in this band gave me more gigging confidence and an experience of recording in a studio. The band was founded by Vinny Morello and Blackbeard Hebburn. The singer/guitarist Alex Morgan joined slightly later and I was last to complete the set. I played my first full band gig with Subtle Culprits.
